02 — About

Behind the lens

I started with a borrowed camera and a roll of film I ruined by opening the back too early. Fifteen years later, the mistake still shapes how I shoot — slowly, deliberately, and with respect for the frame I won't get back.

My work moves between quiet portraits, wide open landscapes, and the unscripted rhythm of the street. What ties it together isn't a subject, it's a way of paying attention.
